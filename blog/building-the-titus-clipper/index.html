Building the Titus Clipper 01/21-2020

The Titus Clipper, 'a floating city, with a city’s worth of architecture' from the Wachoskis Jupiter Ascending

I had so much fun building this massive ship! I spend almost a year working on this model, managing multiple people contributing to it, writing python scripts in order to boosts the model process and support this +billion polygons model.

The good folks over at Framestore posted this article briefly descriping the challenges met on the Jupiter Ascending Movie. A stunningly beautiful, and unfortunately a bad movie. I still haven't managed to actually sit through the entire thing, but it was a fantastic experience to work on.
