Reel

My reel represents an earlier part of my professional life, and I do look back at it with fond memories. I had the pleasure of working on more than a dozen movies in the span of a decade. Some of my most exciting challenges and proudest outcomes have come from working on these projects. While VFX was a rewarding endeavor it feels like it is time to move on to explore other avenues of interest and face new challenges. My work in VFX ranges from Character/Creature Modeling, Shot Modeling, Hard Surface Modeling, Texturing, Environment to Prop Modeling.
You can see an almost complete list of projects I have worked on over at my filmography on IMDb
